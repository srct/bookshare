Privacy Policy

Your information

Listings posted on GMU Bookshare are instantly available to all GMU students, as well as information such as comments and bids made on other user's listings and transaction histories and ratings shown as part of a user's profile. Your real name and email adddress accompany your profile information.

Your profile information is as provided through the university, and we are unable to make any changes to your name, except as well changed by the university.

What data do we collect?

We permanently retain anonymized basic log information on your use of this website via Piwik analytics. Piwick is self-hosted and open source, so your information will not be sold or otherwise transferred to any other third party, nor will your information be otherwise divulged, expect by request of the university or by lawful court order. Ever.

Nothing in this privacy policy should be construed to limit any legal defense or objection to a request or order to disclose your information.

Why do we collect data?

Knowing how many users a service has and how often it gets used tells us how popular that service is.

If a service is getting a lot of use, we will try to provide more support and focus our attention on that service. Our use of your information is limited to improving your experience.

The code for this site is publicly available at our git repository, and you are encouraged to exercise your basic computing freedoms.

Can I opt out?

You may opt out (or in) of analytics.

Your interaction with the site, such as bids, transactions, or ratings, will not be removed, except as involation of GMU's responsible use of computing, or by lawful court order, even upon graduation or other termination of relationship with the university and the cessation of your full access GMU account.

Should any portion of this privacy policy be deemed invalid, illegal, or unenforcable, all other terms of the policy will nevertheless remain in full force and effect.

Because our service requires GMU user account authentication, we do not by design allow minors under the age of 13 to use services.

Our services are housed in Fairfax, Virginia, United States and are subject to law within its jurisdiction. Please contact us with any questions, comments, or concerns.
