About Bookshare

What is Bookshare?

The purpose of Bookshare is to allow Mason students to safely and securely coordinate textbook exchanges.

Have an old textbook? Bookshare will help you find an incoming student who wants to buy it from you.

Who uses Bookshare?

Bookshare is only open to current students and faculty at George Mason University with active NETIDs.

This exclusivity provides a safe, closed community within which users can exchange their textbooks.

Who is Mason SRCT?

Members of the Student-Run Computing and Technology group at George Mason University are the clever, talented students behind Bookshare.

We are a group of creative people that like making software and wanted to give back to the Mason community at the same time.

Contact us to get involved!
