About Bookshare

What is Bookshare?

The purpose of Bookshare is to allow Mason students to safely and securely coordinate textbook exchanges.

Have an old textbook? Bookshare will help you find an incoming student who wants to buy it from you. Need one for the coming semester? Search by ISBN, title, course, and more, or set a lookout to be automatically notified when a textbook you need is posted.

Who uses Bookshare?

Bookshare is only open to current students and faculty at George Mason University with active NETIDs.

This exclusivity provides a safe, closed community within which users can exchange their textbooks.

Who is Mason SRCT?

Members of the Student-Run Computing and Technology group at George Mason University, a registered student organization, are the clever, talented students behind Bookshare.

We are a group of creative people that like making software and wanted to give back to the Mason community at the same time.

Contact us to get involved!

Digital Millennium Copyright Act

Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) compliance on Mason university networks is handled by the George Mason University Copyright Office. George Mason Unversity's Responsible Use of Computing Section IV forbids user violation of copyright laws and licenses. Violators will be subject to punishments including full partitioning from the university network under the STOP IT process.
